Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you arrive?

Our average response time in the Portland Metro area is under 30 minutes. Factors like heavy traffic or severe weather may affect this, but our dispatch team will give you an accurate ETA when you call.

Do you tow heavy-duty vehicles?

Yes, we have a specialized fleet of heavy-duty wreckers equipped to handle semi-trucks, RVs, buses, and commercial heavy equipment safely and efficiently.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve the entire Greater Portland area, including Gresham, Beaverton, Hillsboro, Tigard, Clackamas, and surrounding regions. We also offer long-distance towing statewide.

Are there any hidden fees?

Absolutely not. We pride ourselves on transparent, flat-rate pricing. You will be provided a clear quote before we dispatch a driver, ensuring no surprises.

Do you provide roadside assistance besides towing?

Yes! We offer a full suite of roadside services including battery jump starts, flat tire changes, fuel delivery, vehicle lockouts, and winch-outs.
