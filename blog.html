What to Do When Your Car Overheats on I-84

Published on March 25, 2026 by Flash Team

Driving through Portland traffic when suddenly your temperature gauge spikes? An overheating engine is one of the most stressful roadside emergencies.

1. Pull Over Safely

The moment you notice the temperature rising into the red zone, or see steam coming from the hood, put on your hazard lights and safely pull onto the shoulder.

2. Turn Off the Engine

Do not attempt to push the car "just a few more miles." Turn off the ignition immediately to prevent permanent engine block damage.

3. Call For Help

Never open a hot radiator cap, as boiling coolant can cause severe burns. Stay safely inside your vehicle and call Flash Towing PDX at (971) 326-8952.
